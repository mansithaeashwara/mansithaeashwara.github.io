"Moments of Brilliance" is a short film that imagines Albert Einstein navigating the cultural challenges and parental expectations of growing up in Sri Lanka. Watch the humor unfold as Einstein finds himself in amusing predicaments, highlighting the clash between his genius and the cultural norms of the island. It's a light-hearted take on what might happen when a legendary thinker meets the unique pressures of Sri Lankan life.
